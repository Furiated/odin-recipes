Turkey BLT Sandwich

Description

Turkey Sandwich with Lettuce, Tomato, Bacon, and Mayo

Ingredients

  • 3 slices turkey
  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 1 large lettuce leaf
  • 2 slices microwave bacon
  • 1 bottle Olive Oil Mayo

Steps

  1. Cook bacon per package instructions
  2. Coat inside of bread slices with mayo to taste
  3. Layer on turkey, tomato, and lettuce
  4. Add bacon and close sandwich
